About Us

हमारे बारे में

We are a non-profit NGO working for women empowerment. For the last 4 years, we have been striving to support women through education, skills, and opportunities.

हम एक गैर-लाभकारी संस्था हैं जो महिला सशक्तिकरण के लिए काम कर रही है। पिछले 4 वर्षों से हम शिक्षा, कौशल और अवसरों के माध्यम से महिलाओं को सशक्त बनाने का प्रयास कर रहे हैं।

Our Story

4 years ago, we started "Ek Pahel Esi Bhi" with a simple thought – "If a woman is empowered, the whole family is empowered."

In the beginning, we were only 3–4 members with limited resources...

After 4 years:

  • 200+ women have received training.
  • Self-help groups are now running their small businesses.
  • Awareness drives have spread knowledge about health, education, and legal rights.

Our journey has just begun...
